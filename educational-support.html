Career Guidance Educational Support

Welcome to the Educational Support page! Here are some scholarships you can apply for:

  • National Eagle Scout Association Scholarships

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $10,000

    Be a senior in high school through their junior year in an undergraduate program or by the halfway point of their associate degree program or skilled trade program. Be a member of the National Eagle Scout Association.

  • FOSSI

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $40,000

    Complete or have completed high school with a minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale (unweighted) and be entering their freshman year of college. Attend an HBCU for the entirety of their college education. Have an interest in pursuing a career in chemical manufacturing, engineering, environmental health and sustainability, or other related chemical-industry fields. Demonstrate financial need.

  • Courage to Grow

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $500

    Be a junior or senior in high school. GPA must be a 2.5 or higher.

  • SP Scholarship

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $2,500

    At least 13 years old and enrolled or will enroll in a college or university in the U.S. A legal resident of the U.S or Puerto Rico.

  • SM Scholarship

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $2,000

    A parent who currently has a child enrolled in an accredited college or university located within the United States; A student who is currently enrolled in an accredited college or university located within the United States; A parent who currently has a child enrolled as a high school senior located within the United States; A student who is currently enrolled as a high school junior or senior who intends to enroll in an accredited college or university located within the United States, within a year of graduating high school.

  • Appily Scholarship

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $1,000

    This scholarship is open to high school sophomores, juniors, and first-semester senior students for all majors and schools. We will judge this scholarship based on other criteria such as volunteer work, extracurricular activities, special circumstances, etc.

  • David B. Durkee Memorial Scholarship Program

    Deadline: 01/31/2024

    Amount: $5,000

    Children of BCTGM members who are entering college for the first time in the Fall of 2024. Members who are starting, or returning to college in the Fall of 2024.
